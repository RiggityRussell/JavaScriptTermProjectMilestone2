This is a text based mystery game!
There will be many choices and questions as you work through the game.
Once you have finished you will be naming the KILLER!
You may enter q at any time to quit the game.
You may enter x at any time to return to the Mansion Lobby
Sometimes there may be special hidden things if you try inputs that dont seem available.....
